What is the purpose of Nightlinks?

Nightlinks serves with the mission to help all NYC-area creatures of the night to:

  • Promote your own work, goods, or services.
  • Connect with others in your line of work for advice, collaboration, and references.
  • Find providers for your goods and service needs.

How is Nightlinks different from mainstream professional networking platforms?

Members of our community often have unique advertising needs.

  • You may not want your services associated with your public profile.
  • Your experience and qualifications may be unusual.
  • Your goods and services may be targeted at very specific niches.
  • You may prefer to collaborate with local, more personal connections.

Nightlinks strives to serve these needs.

Who can be listed on Nightlinks?

Nightlinks welcomes all NYC-area creatures of the night:

  • Citizens of New York City and the greater surrounding American northeast area (New York state, Connecticut, New Jersey, etc).
  • Non-northeasterners are welcome to submit listings if you think they will be of interest to members of the northeast community.
  • Members of all dark and alternative scenes (goths, rivetheads, deathrockers, metalheads, punks, and more)
  • And beloved Nightkind creatures of all kinds.

Don't worry if you fit in. If being listed here would serve you or the community, your presence is welcomed.

Does my job or product have to be gothy to be listed?

No! Our goal is to see you succeed in whatever your job is. Careers and products of any kind are welcome.

Do I have to be an experienced professional to be listed?

We welcome entrepreneurs of all experience levels (although we ask that you represent your services honestly if you are very new).
If you are not currently a paid professional, but are still very experienced or knowledgable about a topic and would like to be listed to support others in the area, Nightlinks gratefully welcomes your listing.

Do I have to own my business to be listed?

You do not! If you're an employee, you're still welcome to:

  • List yourself as a member of your industry,
  • List your business with the permission of the owners, and/or
  • Join the Discord community for community support without being listed.

Can I be listed anonymously?

Yes! You are welcome to submit a listing under a pseudonym or username of your choosing.
You can also use contact information such as email or social media profiles that are not associated with your identity.
If you have not yet set up an anonymous form of contact, you may give Nightlinks your contact information privately and ask that any requests for your services be routed through us.

Does Nightlinks screen the people or services listed?

Because anyone can list their work, Nightlinks cannot vet or endorse the character or quality of any of our listings.
You are highly encouraged to do your own research and use your best judgement to find collaborators whose work and values align with your expectations.

What is the Discord? Can I join?

Our Discord server is a social platform where Nightlink members can communicate in private channels just for others in their line of work.
You can request to be added to the Discord here.
You do not need to be listed on Nightlinks to join the Discord community, and you do not need to join the Discord to be listed on Nightlinks. You can do both or just one, whatever serves you.

How do I update my listing?

If you already have a listing but would like to update it, submit a request here.
